One local workspace

From an idea to evidence

Build, explain and test strategies without sending keys or research to a project cloud.

Chart and market data

Custom Canvas, configurable confirmed Renko, Three Line Break, Kagi and Point & Figure, BOS/CHOCH and FVG, anchored/session VWAP, VPVR, depth, footprint/CVD, a Shift-drag ruler and UTC-linked multi-chart zoom/pan.

Strategies and Pine

Blockly authoring plus fail-closed Pine v4–v6 conversion with explicit exact, approximate and unsupported diagnostics.

Auditable backtests

Costs, warm-up, provenance, Monte Carlo, walk-forward and deterministic traces for signals, fills and state changes.

Paper before live

Paper bots by default, durable order journals, exchange reconciliation, risk caps and an emergency stop.

Local-first security

Encrypted exchange keys, local SQLite, scoped access, CSRF protection and no project telemetry.

Tested and inspectable

Type checks, lint, unit/integration/parity tests, browser E2E, generated references and attested release artifacts.
